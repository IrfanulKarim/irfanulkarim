Overview

Extensive experience across a range of industries and products, including Real Acquisitions, Flowcite, Leekim, Monezsoft, iDream, Merchaint, Drive Good, Vutal, Routii, Slikekspressen, and Solactivy. I specialize in taking products from concept to market, leveraging data-driven insights, customer research, and strategic planning to create value. My work spans from defining product goals and building Minimum Viable Products (MVPs) to collaborating with design and engineering teams using Agile methodologies. I ensure seamless product development by creating detailed Product Requirements Documents (PRDs), leading Go-to-Market strategies, and driving post-launch improvements through retrospectives and ongoing optimization. My approach focuses on aligning teams, enhancing processes, and continuously refining products to meet customer needs and business objectives.
